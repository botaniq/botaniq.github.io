  • Open an Ethereum Account

    Buying or selling Ethereum at Bitcoin Alley is as simple as transacting Bitcoin or any of the other digital currencies we have. First we ask you to complete our short and simple account registration process. This information is required to comply with State and Federal laws and to protect all of our users. We will verify the information and then we will provide you with a free account to buy Ethereum. It's that easy.

  • Fund your Account

    Now that you have an account at BitcoinAlley the second step in the process is to choose how you will fund your account. You can purchase Ethereum with debit cards or directly from your bank account using secure ACH transactions. Once you have connected a payment method we will then provide you with your own personal digital wallet to store your Ethereum. This digital wallet is secure and only you will have the password to buy and sell your Ethereum.

  • Buying and Selling
    Ethereum

    Are you ready to start buying and selling Ethereum? Now just go to our home page and under the header there are drop down menus to Buy or Sell Cryptocurrencies. Click on the appropriate tab for your transaction and then choose Ethereum. Fill in the amount you wish to buy or sell and click enter. A pop up confirmation will appear to confirm the amounts before your order is made. This is an added layer of protection so you can be sure all the desired amounts you want to trade are correct.

  • Controlled Secure
    Transactions

    BitcoinAlley will transact with your source of funds and your secure digital wallet to complete the transfers necessary to fulfill your transaction. Our system automatically transfers all funds and coins necessary per your trade confirmation. We can buy and sell Ethereum securely from your personal wallet at BitcoinAlley. We can also complete orders using another wallet of yours outside of our platform. We give you many different options when using BitcoinAlley. Most importantly you remain in control at all times.
